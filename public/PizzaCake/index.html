PizzaCake!

Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza crust on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, and olives on top.
  5. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy your delicious Pizza Cake!
